MapleStory is a free-to-play, 2D side-scrolling MMORPG, developed by Wizet (Nexon). As a player of the game, fan art was regularly drawn for personal endeavors and to enter competitions that promoted the game.

Later on, this became a motivator during the hiring process at Nexon and eventually I worked on collaborations with Maplestory across Nexon M's game titles.
