Legacy Quest

  • Nexon M

  • Discontinued in May, 2017

  • 2014 - 2015


Legacy Quest was a free-to-play, action RPG, rogue-like, dungeon crawler mobile game. It was developed by Socialspiel Entertainment and published by Nexon M. It was released for both iOS and Android.

During my time with Legacy Quest, I created static ads and marketing email campaigns during the game's closed beta launch.
