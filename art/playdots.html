  • Dots & Kickstarter collaboration

'Connected by Dots' was a marketing, branding initiative originally started to support indie creators by promoting their projects similar to the company's founding with the support of Betaworks. Although the initiative never went live, it was an exciting opportunity to branch out with Kickstarter to support projects outside of gaming.
