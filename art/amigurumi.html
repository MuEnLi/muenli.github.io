Amigurumi - Assassin's Creed

  • Personal

  • 2012

Personal Project - Japanese style crochet of Sackboy, from the game Little Big Planet, in Assassin's Creed Costume.


Design Process

The crochet pattern was written from the ground up following the visual aesthetic of the Sackboy character. The Assassin's Creed costume was hand sewn, and foam was used to create the accessories.
