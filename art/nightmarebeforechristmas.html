360 BOOK SERIES

Nightmare before Christmas

  • Personal

  • 2015

Papercrafting project inspired by Yusuke Oono's 360 books using similar techniques before mass production.

Nightmare Before Christmas, each, was a 40 page 360 book directly referenced to Tim's Burton's 1993 film, The Nightmare before Christmas. Constructed with heavy premium grade white and black cardstock in Japanese style binding and rotates 360 degrees to form a panoramic 3D paper sculpture.

It comes in 2 different variations: Black and white, White and Black. The black version has a white cover and depicts Jack on the hill. The white version has a black cover and depicts Jack and Sally on the hill.
