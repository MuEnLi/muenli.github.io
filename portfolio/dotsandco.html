Released in 2016, Dots & co was the spiritual successor of Two Dots with characters providing power-ups to the user as they journey through different worlds in the game. While the game no longer releases content, it is still live to play in iOS and Google Play for free.

As the project was de-prioritized, I was also tasked with converting the rasterized map content assets to vector and importing them to Unity.
